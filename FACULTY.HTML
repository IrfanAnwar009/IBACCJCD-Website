    PRINCIPAL

    Irfan Ali Lashari

    (IBA Community College Jacobabad)


    IBA Community College Jacobabad is a beacon of hope which imparts civilizing influence upon the character building of community of Jacobabad in special and others in general. The community college of Jacobabad is one of the outshoots of IBA Sukkur whose mission behind the establishment of Community College is dynamic, inspiring, innovative, cooperative and collaborative in learning curve which marks the turning point in real man of education.IBA Community College Jacobabadis a nursery where in busy and friendly community of dedicated team of teacherscoupled with sound scheme of education and cooperative learning followed in this institution is not only committed to inspire creativity and critical thinking of new learning lot but also focused to nurture intellectual curiosity and innovative abilities of students through interactive class room sessions and technology integrated student learning.
    This institution aims to produce the intellectual army of learning lot which I believe, after passing out from this College will uphold dignity and honour of this College by playing the role of honest,competent,and dedicated leaders in all the departments of Country.
    I am privileged to be able under the unstinting support and captaincy of honourable Prof. Nisar Ahmed Siddique,Director IBA Sukkur, to develop the strengths of all students and then foster a will to use these strengths in the pursuit of excellence for the benefit of college, themselves,community,and above all the country.
    I welcome all the students who aspire to shape their future in this seat of learning, and wish them every success.
