From 21st to 23rd January,2016, Parent-Teacher meeting was held in Middle, Primary and Senior sections receptively. Whereby Mid Term result of the students was announced and shared with the parents.

On 16th December,2015, Students of IBA Community college jacobabad commemorated the great martyrs of suicidal attack on "Army Public School Peshawar" a year back.Speeches were delivered by both teachers and students aimed the illuminated candles and acknowledgement of gratitude was presented. In the last, the hands were raised in prayers for the eradication of terrorism from the country.
